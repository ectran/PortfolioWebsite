SNTRUGGLED

Gameplay Programmer • VR Systems

Unity C# VR FPS Research Lab

OVERVIEW

SNTRUGGLED is a VR first-person survival shooter developed for Cal Poly Pomona’s VR research lab. Players navigate an interactive warehouse environment, collect resources, and survive waves of enemies using motion-based combat and spatial interaction systems.

PROJECT INTENT

This project explores VR interaction design within a research context, focusing on physical immersion, interaction clarity, and combat usability in a first-person VR environment. The goal was to evaluate how traditional FPS mechanics translate into embodied player movement.
