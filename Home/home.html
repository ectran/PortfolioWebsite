ABOUT

Hi there! I’m Emily Tran, an aspiring game developer and artist with a passion for creating all kinds of games (though I do have a soft spot for 2D, narrative-driven adventure games). I’m currently in my final year at Cal Poly Pomona, studying Computer Science, where I’m combining my love for programming and the creative arts to bring games to life.

This site is where I keep my personal projects, artworks, and contact info. Consider this site in “early access”—it’ll keep getting patches, updates, and new content, just like me. :)

MEET MY OC

Meet Vee, my personal original character (OC) and digital persona! While her appearance can be dark and scary, her personality is quite the opposite. Vee is known as the quiet and mysterious girl, but those close know her to be incredibly innocent, warm, and sweet… also secretly prone to obsessive fixations. Her overall design reflects elements of my own personality, allowing me to share a piece of myself through my art.
