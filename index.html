ABOUT

Heyo! I'm Emily, a game developer and 2D artist passionate about building games with strong narratives and innovative mechanics. I enjoy working and experimenting across many genres, but I specialize in 2D games and pixel art, which is the style I most often use in my projects.

This site is where I keep my projects, artwork, and contact info updated. Enjoy browsing! :)

MEET MY OC

Meet Vee, my personal original character (OC) and digital persona! While her appearance may seem dark or intimidating, her personality is quite the opposite. She’s often seen as quiet and mysterious, but those close to her know she’s incredibly warm, innocent, and sweet… though she may also have a tendency for intense, obsessive fixations.

Her design reflects pieces of my own personality, allowing me to share a little bit of myself through my art!
