ABOUT ME

Heyo! I’m Emily, a gameplay programmer and 2D artist who builds systems-driven games where mechanics, visuals, and feel all work together.

I’m most interested in the space between art and engineering: where code shapes movement, animation supports decision-making, and UI quietly changes how a player thinks. I usually work in Unity, building gameplay systems, prototyping mechanics, and creating 2D/pixel art and animation that support the overall player experience. I enjoy collaborating with others, picking up new skills, and wearing different hats when a project calls for it.

This site is a collection of my game projects, creative experiments, and technical work in gameplay systems, AI, and interactive design. Thanks for stopping by :)
