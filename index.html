ABOUT ME

Heyo! I'm Emily, a gameplay programmer, technical artist, and computer science graduate from Cal Poly Pomona who enjoys building systems where code, design, and creativity intersect.

My work focuses on gameplay programming, AI behaviors, progression systems, and player-facing mechanics in Unity. I enjoy exploring how small systems interact to create meaningful player experiences, whether that's adaptive difficulty, risk/reward economies, VR interactions, or combat mechanics.

Outside of programming, I also create 2D art, animation, and UI assets, which gives me a broader perspective on how gameplay, visuals, and player feedback come together. Some of my favorite projects have come from experimenting with ideas that weren't part of the original plan, turning unexpected discoveries into mechanics, features, or entirely new directions.

I'm always looking to collaborate with people who enjoy building things and pushing ideas further than they started. Feel free to reach out! :)
