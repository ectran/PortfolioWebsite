FITCH

Unity 2D Visual Style Exploration Animation Prototype

Fitch is a 2D visual and animation prototype created to explore character movement, sprite animation, and art direction for a Metroidvania-inspired game. Players control Fitch, a small adventurer accompanied by a fox companion capable of granting unique abilities.

This solo project focuses on experimenting with animation pipelines, visual style development, and responsive character movement while establishing the foundation for a potential larger game project.
