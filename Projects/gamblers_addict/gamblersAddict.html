GAMBLERS ADDICT

Unity C# 2D Bullet Hell Boss Rush Risk/Reward Systems Prototype

Gamblers Addict is a bullet hell boss prototype exploring risk and reward gameplay systems. Players face a powerful boss encounter while placing bets on their own performance, wagering resources to increase potential rewards while raising the stakes of each battle.

This solo prototype was created to experiment with scoring systems, difficulty balancing, and player-driven risk mechanics within fast-paced bullet hell combat. The project served as a technical exploration of combat design and gameplay feedback systems.
