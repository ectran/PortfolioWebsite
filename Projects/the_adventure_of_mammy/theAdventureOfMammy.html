THE ADVENTURE OF MAMMY

Unity C# 2D Soulslike Metroidvania Combat Systems Prototype

The Adventure of Mammy is a 2D action prototype exploring combat and movement systems inspired by Soulslike and Metroidvania games. Players navigate interconnected environments while managing stamina, performing evasive rolls, and engaging enemies through timing-based combat.

This project was developed independently as a technical exploration of character control, combat responsiveness, and system-driven gameplay design. The prototype focuses on refining player movement, attack mechanics, and core combat feedback.
