FUNK VS FRIGHT

Unity C# 2.5D Rhythm Game Game Jam 1st Place Playable

FUNK VS FRIGHT is a 2.5D rhythm combat game and the 1st Place winner of the 2025 Halloween Horror Game Jam hosted by SPIN Game Jam. Players take control of FUNK, a girl navigating a strange and unsettling world where battles are fought through perfectly timed musical attacks. As players progress, they encounter eerie characters and uncover fragments of a deeper story surrounding the world and FUNK's identity.

Developed by a team of four over the course of ten days, the project focused on blending rhythm mechanics with cinematic combat presentation. I served as the project lead, technical artist, and UI & narrative designer, coordinating development while implementing gameplay visuals, interface systems, and story elements.
