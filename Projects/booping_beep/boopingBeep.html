BOOPING BEEP

Unity C# 2D Endless Runner Game Jam Playable WebGL

Booping Beep is a fast-paced 2D endless runner created for the 2025 Spring Game Jam hosted by SPIN Game Jam, GDC Northridge, and VGDA. Players control a bee named Beep as they weave through an increasingly chaotic stream of obstacles while collecting power-ups and chasing high scores.

Developed by a team of four within a 75-hour time limit, the game focuses on quick reactions and arcade-style gameplay inspired by classic mobile runners. I served as design lead and technical artist, contributing to gameplay systems, visual polish, and overall game feel.
