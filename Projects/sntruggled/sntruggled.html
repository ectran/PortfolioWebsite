SNTRUGGLED

Unity C# VR FPS Research Lab

Sntruggled is a VR first-person shooter developed for Cal Poly Pomona’s research VR Lab. Players explore an immersive warehouse environment where they search for supplies, open chests, and survive waves of deceptively cute but dangerous enemies using motion-based combat and spatial interactions.

The game was developed by a team of four over the course of four months as part of a research-focused VR development program. During development, the project was showcased to public audiences where players tested the experience and provided feedback that helped shape the final iteration of the game. I worked as a gameplay programmer focusing on core combat systems and VR interaction mechanics.
